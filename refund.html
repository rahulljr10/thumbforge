Refund Policy

Effective June 21, 2026

Subscriptions

You may cancel future renewal at any time. Charges for a current billing period are generally non-refundable once production capacity or credits have been used.

Unused purchases

If a duplicate or accidental charge is reported promptly and no associated credits were used, we will review it in good faith.

Missed brief

A direction that clearly fails the agreed brief will be revised or replaced without an additional credit. Creative preference changes outside the agreed brief may require a new credit.

Top-ups

Unused top-up credits may be eligible for review within 7 days of purchase. Used or partially used top-ups are not refundable.

Requesting review

Submit the account email, charge date and reason through the contact page. Approved refunds return to the original payment method.
