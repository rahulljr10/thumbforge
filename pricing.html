What counts as one credit?

One finished thumbnail concept delivered for review counts as one credit.

What happens when I reach zero?

You can add any number of credits for $3 each or wait for your normal monthly renewal.

Do revisions cost credits?

No. One reasonable revision is included with each video brief.

Can I move between plans?

Yes. Upgrade when your upload schedule grows or move back at your next billing cycle.
