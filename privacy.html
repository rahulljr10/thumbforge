Privacy Policy

Effective June 21, 2026

Information collected

We collect account details, country, channel preferences, submitted briefs, uploaded files, project activity, support requests, transaction records and basic security logs needed to operate ThumbForge.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate accounts, provide thumbnail production, maintain credits, deliver files, answer support requests, secure the service and improve reliability.

Service providers

ThumbForge uses infrastructure providers for authentication, database hosting, private file storage, email and payments. Providers process information only for their contracted role.

Submitted creative material

Scripts, footage, references and briefs remain private to your account and authorized production personnel. They are not sold. Public portfolio use requires separate permission.

Retention

Account and transaction data is retained while needed for service, security and legal obligations. You may request account deletion, subject to required financial record retention.

Security

We use authenticated access, row-level database policies and private storage. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may update account preferences, opt out of product updates and request access, correction or deletion through the contact page.

International processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than your own. Appropriate provider safeguards are used where required.

Updates

Material policy changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.
